MeMindHope was created in 2020 during the pandemic with the main objective of helping people who are struggling with mental health issues. The pandemic was not easy for anyone; it was a very challenging period where we all had to adapt to isolation and losses. We observed that depression, anxiety, and other mental health problems increased over the years. We want to help treat this illness because we know that it can cause pain and even risk lives. If you are struggling, know that you are not alone, and we are here to help you.







Don't fight alone
in this battle





What do we offer to the society?




We offer treatment to the following:

•  Anxiety

•  Depression

•  Bipolar affective disorder

•  Dissociation and dissociative disorders

•  Eating disorders

•  Schizophrenia

•  Paranoia

•  Post-traumatic stress disorder







Your life is important!
Know that is okay to ask for help.
